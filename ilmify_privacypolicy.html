Privacy Policy for Ilmify

Effective Date: 29 December 2025

1. About the App

Ilmify is a platform that connects students looking for home tuition with teachers offering paid teaching services. The app enables users to find, contact, and meet each other for educational purposes.

2. Information We Collect

Teacher Information (For Security Verification)

  • Full name
  • CNIC number
  • CNIC image
  • Live photograph for identity verification
  • Basic contact and teaching-related details

This information is collected strictly for verification and security purposes.

Student Information

  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Location (optional)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Verify teacher identities
  • Ensure safety and trust on the platform

  • Prevent fraud or misuse

We do NOT:

  • Use data for analytics or research
  • Share data for commercial purposes
