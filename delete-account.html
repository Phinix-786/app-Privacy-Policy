Delete Your Ilmify Account

If you wish to delete your Ilmify account and all associated personal data, please follow the instructions below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Ilmify app and go to your profile/settings page.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your request, we will delete:

  • Profile information (name, email, contact info)

Data Retention

Some anonymized system logs may be retained for up to 90 days for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes. No personal identifying information will be kept beyond account deletion.
